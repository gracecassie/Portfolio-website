A Little About Myself...

My name is Grace Cassie and I'm passionate about improving the lives of others through design. I'm constantly learning new things everyday and love meeting people who are motivated and unafraid of sharing their ideas with the world.

I consider myself a "jack of all trades" and find myself longing to explore various mediums. From oil painting to digital design, I love to see things slowly be created, which is why I have also found a passion for UX/UI Design.
