A Little About Myself...

My name is Grace, and I am currently a student at Louisiana State University studying Graphic Design. As a creative individual with a passion for technology and design, I am actively seeking internship opportunities in the field of web development.

My interest in front-end web development started when I realized that I wanted to combine my passion for design with technology. Since discovering this interest, I have been furthering the skills taught to me at Lousiana State University by teaching myself key programming languages. This site serves as an example of my knowledge in HTML and CSS thus far, and I hope to continue learning on my own and through others.

I have created this portfolio website to showcase my skills and experience and to provide an overview of the projects that I have worked on. If you are interested in learning more about my experience, skills, or have any opportunities available, please feel free to contact me. Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio website!
